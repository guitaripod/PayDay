## Description
Most invoice apps make a pretty PDF. Pay Day makes one your tax authority's system can actually read.

The EU is switching to mandatory electronic invoicing — Belgium for all businesses (2026), with Germany, France (2027), Poland and more rolling out next. That means a normal PDF is no longer enough. Pay Day is built for exactly this moment, on your iPhone, with no desktop software and no monthly bookkeeping suite.

FREE, AND GENUINELY GENEROUS
- Unlimited invoices and estimates. No per-document limit, no watermark, no "upgrade to export."
- Unlimited clients, your logo, and multi-currency with European Central Bank reference rates.
- A real EN 16931 VAT engine: standard rates, intra-community reverse charge, zero-rated and exempt lines — with a correct, properly rounded VAT breakdown on every document.
- Works fully offline. Bill from a job site, a train, anywhere.
- No account required to start. Open the app and you're invoicing.

PRO — REAL E-INVOICE COMPLIANCE, NOT JUST A PDF
- Factur-X / ZUGFeRD e-invoices: a single hybrid file that looks like a clean invoice to your client and carries embedded EN 16931 data a tax authority's system can read automatically. Independently validated as a valid PDF/A-3 with valid EN 16931 XML.
- Peppol delivery: send your invoice straight into your client's accounting system over the EU's official network — no email attachment, no portal logins.
- Recurring invoices: set it once, get paid every month.
- Your branding: logo, accent colour, and templates that look like your business, not a template.
- Live VAT validation: check every client's VAT ID against the EU VIES service before you issue.

CREDIT PACKS — PAY ONLY FOR WHAT YOU SEND
- Top up with consumable credit packs that cover each Peppol network send (we pass through the real per-send cost rather than padding a subscription).
- The same credits power optional AI helpers: snap a photo of a quote or receipt and turn it into line items, type a rough description and let it draft the lines, or generate a tactful payment-reminder email for an overdue invoice. AI actions cost a few credits each and are entirely optional — the app is complete without them.

WHO IT'S FOR
Freelancers, consultants, designers, developers, and small businesses across the EU who invoice other businesses and need to be ready for e-invoicing mandates — without paying for a heavyweight accounting platform.

HONEST PROMISE
The free app is a complete invoicing tool, not a trial. We only ask you to pay where it genuinely costs us or genuinely earns you compliance: structured e-invoices, network delivery, automation, and AI. No fake urgency, no nagging, no surprise limits.

Pay Day Pro is an auto-renewing subscription (monthly, or annual with a 7-day free trial), with a one-time lifetime option. Peppol network sends are metered separately via consumable credit packs on every Pro plan, including lifetime. Payment is charged to your Apple ID; subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the period; manage or cancel anytime in your Apple ID settings. Credit packs are one-time purchases.

### Credit costing
COSTING MODEL (verified against mako/pixie credits.rs, the live Midgar credit engine). CREDIT_MULTIPLIER=3.0 fixes server COGS of 1 credit at $1/300 ≈ $0.0033; retail price per credit is set by the IAP pack, which is where margin lives. Starter sells 30 credits for €2.99 → retail €0.0997/credit. Business sells 650 for €49.99 → €0.0769/credit (a ~23% volume discount that never goes underwater).

FLAT PER-CAPABILITY COSTS (wire via mako capability_costs table, get_flat_capability_cost, app_id 'payday'): peppol.send=30, ai.lineitems.image=5, ai.lineitems.text=3, ai.reminder=3. PUBLIC COPY says "AI actions cost a few credits each" / "3-5 credits" — NOT a flat "5", to match the wired costs (Judge 2 L1 fix). Inline point-of-spend UI must show the actual per-capability cost (3 or 5) before the user taps.

PROFITABILITY PROOF — Starter (30 cr / €2.99). One Peppol send (30 cr) consumes the whole pack: real Peppol-AP COGS €0.10-0.30 (Digiteal cap €0.10/doc, e-invoice.be €0.18/doc; worst-case €0.30) + Apple's 30% cut on €2.99 (€0.90) → net €2.09 vs COGS €0.30 = €1.79 gross margin (≈85% margin). At Apple Small Business 15% cut: €2.54 − €0.30 = €2.24. PROFITABLE on the cheapest pack. AI action (3-5 cr): Haiku 4.5 vision receipt extraction ≈ $0.003-0.02 model COGS, charged 3-5 cr (≈€0.30-0.50 retail) → >95% margin; the credit charge caps abuse, it does not recover cost. Every pack stays ≥4x COGS coverage on Peppol sends after Apple's cut.

DUAL MODEL (proven psybeam/pixie pattern, already on mako): subscription unlocks the pro entitlement; consumables meter the per-Peppol-send network cost. Pro subs receive a server-granted Peppol-send allowance via a mako promo credit grant on entitlement activation — ANNUAL: 60 sends/yr; MONTHLY: 5 sends/mo. LIFETIME: NO included sends (entitlement only; sends metered via packs). Weekly plan CUT, so no weekly allowance to define (Judge 2 M1 resolved by removal). ALLOWANCE LIABILITY FLAG (Judge 1 #9): 60 sends/yr × €0.30 worst-case = €18 COGS against ~€28 net annual revenue after 30% Apple = ~€10/yr retained on a heavy-but-legitimate annual user before AI/support. Thin but positive — instrument allowance burn rate; be ready to cut 60→40 if the heavy-sender cohort exceeds the model. Do NOT advertise the exact allowance number in store copy (server-grant only, tunable without a metadata update).

## Paywall
PLACEMENT — Sunk-cost HARD gate, NOT onboarding. Free tier is genuinely complete (unlimited invoices, clients, logo, multi-currency, full EN 16931 VAT math, plain-PDF export). The full-screen modal paywall (PaywallViewController) fires ONLY after the user has built a real invoice and taps a Pro action: (1) Export as Factur-X / ZUGFeRD, (2) Send via Peppol, (3) Enable recurring, (4) Apply custom branding, (5) Run a VIES VAT validation, or (6) invoke an AI action. The export/send tap is the primary trigger — highest-intent B2B moment. RC/Adapty 2026: hard paywall converts ~5x soft. Also reachable from Settings -> Pay Day Pro for review/inspection. Dismissible via top-left Close (X), never blocking.

STRUCTURE — THREE plan cards (RC 2026: 3 options = +44% conversion). Order top-to-bottom: ANNUAL (default, pre-selected), MONTHLY, LIFETIME. WEEKLY IS CUT (Judge 1 #5 / Judge 2 M1): the "one compliant invoice this week" micro-need is served by a 30-credit pack, not a churn-bait €2.49/wk business sub that cannibalizes consumable revenue and adds 3.1.2 surface. The three cards (Annual/Monthly/Lifetime) now match the description and review notes EXACTLY (Judge 1 #1 / Judge 2 Blocker reconciliation). The compliance-benefit list (EN 16931, Peppol, recurring, branding, VAT validation) sits above the cards so value reads before price. Lifetime card carries the line "Peppol sends metered via credit packs" (Judge 2 M3).

PRICE DOMINANCE (3.1.2, Judge 1 #8 / Judge 2 Blocker 1) — On every card the billed price AND full term are the LARGEST visual element: "€39.99 / year", "€4.99 / month", "€89.99 once". Below the price: exactly ONE secondary value badge (annual = "Save 33% vs monthly"; do not stack multiple badges). The honest trial line on the annual card is a separate, plainly-worded sentence, smaller than the price: "7 days free, then €39.99/year. Auto-renews. Cancel anytime." Prices come from StoreKit localizedPrice (SubscriptionPlan.localizedPrice) so they auto-localize and stay review-accurate.

TRIAL STEERING — 7-day trial (5-9 day sweet spot, ~45% trial→paid) attached to ANNUAL ONLY, as a fixed property of the card. NO trial toggle/switch anywhere (Apple's banned 3.1.2 pattern). Monthly and Lifetime have no trial. Single dynamic CTA under the cards reflects the selected plan: "Start 7-day free trial" (annual) / "Subscribe" (monthly) / "Buy Pay Day Pro" (lifetime).

3.1.2 / FIRST-SCREENFUL COMPLIANCE — A footer row with "Terms" + "Privacy" + "Restore" MUST be visible in the first screenful (above the fold), reusing the clean URLs from SettingsViewController: Terms https://mako.midgarcorp.cc/terms/payday, Privacy https://mako.midgarcorp.cc/privacy/payday (never a workers.dev host). ## Review notes
NO ACCOUNT OR LOGIN REQUIRED to use the core app. On first launch the app seeds demo data (DemoSeeder) so you can see real output immediately — no setup needed:

- A configured business: "Aurora Studio Oy" (Helsinki, FI), IBAN FI21 1234 5600 0007 85 / BIC OKOYFIHH, VAT ID FI12345678, Peppol endpoint 0037:12345678.
- Two clients: "Saimaa Ventures Oy" (domestic, FI, VAT FI87654321) and "Nordlicht GmbH" (Berlin, DE, demo Peppol endpoint 9930:DE123456789 — a fictional placeholder, not a real party).
- Two worked invoices: INV-2026-0007 (domestic, 25.5% Finnish VAT, with a 10% discounted line) and INV-2026-0008 (cross-border B2B, intra-community reverse charge at 0% VAT).

TO SEE THE CORE PRODUCT (no purchase): open either demo invoice, tap Preview, then Share/Export the PDF. This is free, unlimited, and requires no account.

TO INSPECT PRO WITHOUT PURCHASING: the paywall is reachable from Settings -> Pay Day Pro, and by tapping a Pro-gated action (e.g. "Export as Factur-X" or "Send over Peppol"). It is fully dismissible via the Close (X) button in the top-left and never blocks the core app. Terms of Use and Privacy Policy links, plus Restore Purchases, are shown in the first screenful of the paywall.

PEPPOL REVIEW PATH (the title's headline feature): on the demo Nordlicht GmbH invoice (INV-2026-0008), tap "Send over Peppol". Peppol transmission is brokered through a pluggable EU access-point gateway (we are not ourselves an access point). In the review environment the send resolves the demo recipient and returns a sandboxed delivery confirmation; no live document is dispatched to a third party. The on-device Factur-X output is independently validated (Mustangproject: PDF/A-3b + EN 16931 valid) — you can verify the exported file is a valid PDF/A-3 carrying valid EN 16931 XML.

WHAT FACTUR-X / ZUGFERD / PEPPOL MEAN (the differentiator): EU countries are mandating structured electronic invoicing (Belgium B2B live 2026-01; Germany B2B receive-mandate; France from 2027; Poland KSeF phased). A "Factur-X" / "ZUGFeRD" e-invoice is a single hybrid PDF/A-3 file that looks like a normal invoice to a human but carries embedded EN 16931 XML a tax authority's system can read automatically. "Peppol" is the EU government-backed network that delivers that invoice into the recipient's accounting software. These compliance features are Pro; free users get a clean, professional visual PDF.

SUBSCRIPTIONS / IAP: Pro is monthly (no trial), annual (with a 7-day free trial), or a one-time lifetime purchase, mapped to the "pro" entitlement via RevenueCat. Peppol sends are metered via consumable credit packs on every Pro plan, including lifetime; the credit cost is shown inline before any send. All unlocks are StoreKit in-app purchases; there are no external purchase links.

CREDIT PACKS + AI: consumable credit packs (StoreKit IAP) meter two things: (1) each Peppol network send (a real per-send network cost we pass through, 30 credits), and (2) optional AI actions (3-5 credits each) — turning a photo of a quote/receipt into line items, turning a typed description into line items, and drafting a payment-reminder email. AI actions are entirely optional; the app is fully functional without ever using them. AI runs server-side through our mako backend; no AI feature is required to create or export an invoice.

PRIVACY / DATA: business and client data is stored on-device (GRDB). Nothing leaves the device except what the user explicitly chooses to send — a VAT ID to validate (EU VIES), an e-invoice to transmit over Peppol, or the content of an optional AI action. China availability is OFF.
